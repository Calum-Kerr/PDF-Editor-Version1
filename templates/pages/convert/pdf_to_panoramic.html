How It Works
  • Upload your PDF file
  • Choose horizontal or vertical stitching
  • Optionally select specific pages to include
  • The output will be a new PDF with pages stitched together
Tips for Best Results
  • For large documents, consider selecting just a few pages to include
  • Horizontal stitching works best for documents with similar page heights
  • Vertical stitching works best for documents with similar page widths
  • Quality is preserved, but very large panoramas might increase file size
